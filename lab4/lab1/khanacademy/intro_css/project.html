About the islands

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean. It is famous for its crystal-clear water, white sandy beaches, and peaceful atmosphere.

The country is made up of over a thousand small islands, but only about 200 are inhabited. Many islands are private resorts, making the Maldives perfect for relaxation and luxury travel.
